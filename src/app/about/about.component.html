Our History

Founded in 1929, EliteHome Essentials has a rich heritage rooted in excellence and family values. Our journey began with Sanitary Plumbing, a third-generation family business dedicated to providing top-quality plumbing services to the community.

In 1992, we embarked on a significant expansion with the acquisition of Calray Gas Heat Corp. This strategic move allowed us to broaden our service portfolio and solidify our position as a leader in the home services industry.

In 2024, as part of our commitment to growth and innovation in the digital era, we expanded our presence into the online home service market. Introducing two new additions to our brand family - Antler specializing in motors and pumps, and Bolt offering comprehensive electrical services - we aimed to meet the evolving needs of our customers in the digital age. This strategic move further enhanced our ability to provide convenient and reliable home services online, solidifying our reputation as a comprehensive home service provider in the internet world.
