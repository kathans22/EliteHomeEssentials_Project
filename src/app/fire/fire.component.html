5-Year Residential Flow Test Scheduled:

A comprehensive flow test conducted every five years to ensure the proper operation and efficiency of residential fire sprinkler systems. This test verifies water flow, pressure, and system functionality, ensuring your fire suppression system is ready to protect your home in case of an emergency.


Diagnostic Visit (Trip Charge plus 1 hour onsite):

A professional diagnostic service that includes a trip charge and the first hour of onsite inspection. Our licensed technician will assess and troubleshoot issues with your plumbing, heating, or electrical systems, providing a detailed diagnosis and recommendations for repairs or maintenance.


Fire Suppression (Monthly Visual Inspections):

Regular monthly visual inspections of fire suppression systems to ensure all components are in good working condition. This service includes checking for signs of wear, damage, or obstruction, and ensuring that the system is ready to activate in the event of a fire.
